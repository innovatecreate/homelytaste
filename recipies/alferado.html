Fettuccine Alfredo

Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish made with fettuccine pasta, butter, Parmesan cheese, and cream. It is a rich and creamy dish that is perfect for a special occasion.

To make Fettuccine Alfredo, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

To make the sauce, melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the Parmesan cheese and cream and whisk until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package directions. Drain the pasta and add it to the saucepan with the sauce. Toss to coat.

Serve immediately.
