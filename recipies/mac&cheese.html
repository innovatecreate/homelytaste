Mac and Cheese Recipe

This is a recipe for Mac and Cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook macaroni according to package directions.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Add flour and whisk until smooth.
  4. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper until melted.
  7. Drain macaroni and add to cheese sauce.
  8. Mix well and pour into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
