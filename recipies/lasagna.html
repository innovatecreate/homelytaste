Lasagna

This classic Italian dish is made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. It's perfect for a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 (10 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  5. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles.
  7. Spread with another layer of the meat sauce mixture.
  8. Repeat layers until all of the ingredients are used.
  9. Cover the lasagna with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy!
