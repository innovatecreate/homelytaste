Mozzarella Sticks

These mozzarella sticks are crispy and cheesy! They're perfect for a party or a casual meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a shallow bowl, combine the flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a separate shallow bowl, beat the egg.
  4. In a third shallow bowl, combine the breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Dip the mozzarella cubes in the flour mixture, then the egg mixture, then the breadcrumb mixture.
  6. Place the mozzarella sticks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake the mozzarella sticks for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly.
  8. Serve immediately with marinara sauce for dipping.
