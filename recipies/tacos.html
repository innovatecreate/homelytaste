Mexican Tacos

These Mexican tacos are delicious and easy to make! They're perfect for a casual meal or a party.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (1.25 ounces) taco seasoning mix
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheddar cheese, for serving
  • Sour cream, for serving
  • Lettuce, for serving
  • Tomatoes, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and green pepper and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, taco seasoning mix, and 1/2 cup water.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Warm the corn tortillas in the microwave or in a skillet over medium heat.
  6. To assemble the tacos, spread some of the beef mixture on each tortilla.
  7. Top with shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, lettuce, and tomatoes.
  8. Serve immediately.
