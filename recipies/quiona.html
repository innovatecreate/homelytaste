Quinoa Bowl

These bowls are made with quinoa, vegetables, and a protein of your choice. They're a healthy and delicious meal option.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can corn, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa, water, and vegetable broth.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked through.
  3. While the quinoa is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the onion, carrot, and celery to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the black beans, corn, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper to the skillet and cook for 2 minutes, or until heated through.
  6. To assemble the bowls, divide the quinoa evenly among 4 bowls.
  7. Top with the vegetable mixture and serve immediately.

Enjoy!
